Tia's Biography

Tia Sandman began her pre-professional training in Pawleys Island, SC under the direction of Ilka Doubek at Litchfield Dance Arts Academy. In 2011, she relocated to Greenville, SC to further her dance training and academic studies at the South Carolina Governor's School for the Arts and Humanities (SCGSAH), with Stanislav Issaev as her primary instructor. Throughout her time at SCGSAH, Tia also attended summer intensives across the country – Charlotte Ballet (2011), American Ballet Theater (NYC; 2012), Alonzo King LINES Ballet (2011/2013), and Ballet West (2013/2014). In 2013, Tia was offered a scholarship position as a Trainee in Ballet West’s Professional Training Division. Here, she performed in company productions of Swan Lake, The Sleeping Beauty, Giselle, The Nutcracker, Aladdin, and The Little Mermaid.

Most recently, Tia travelled to Pittsburgh, PA to dance professionally with Texture Contemporary Ballet in their summer production, Unmeasured Rhythm. She performed a mixed repertoire choreographed by Alan Obuzor, Kelsey Bartman, and Annalee Traylor; styles included classical ballet, contemporary, and tap. Tia also worked with Wilder Productions (Aspen, CO) in their multimedia and multidisciplinary performance of Nu-topia: Tour the Mystery – an entirely Beatles themed adventure utilizing audio/visual and performative talents. Throughout the year, she participated in workshops held by Hubbard Street Dance Chicago (Spring Intensive 2018) and SALT Contemporary Dance (LINK Dance Festival 2018).

Currently, Tia is completing both a BFA in Ballet and a BS in Environmental Biology from the University of Utah. Here, she has worked personally with Michele Wiles, Roderick George, Penny Saunders, Pablo Piantino, Melissa Bobick, Maggie Wright-Tesch, and Jan Clark-Fugit. She will graduate in May 2019.

Tia is driven to pursue a career in performance and choreography where her intelligence, dependability, and creative nature will allow her to thrive in the challenge of her dream profession.
